Human-centered technology
to support the well-being of
people and planet


In the Empower Lab, we conduct research to design, engineer, and evaluate technology to
promote human wellbeing as well as the wellness of the natural environment on which all life depends.

We specifically focus on developing interactive, data-driven tools that empower people to
individually and collectively create, make sense of, and act upon information, with an emphasis on societally impactful applications in health, education, civics, and sustainability.

Our website is still under construction but will be updated soon with more information about the lab's members, projects, and opportunities for involvement.
In the meantime, please visit the website of lab director Prof. Murnane for details about the sorts of research we do.
